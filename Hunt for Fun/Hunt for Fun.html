Hunt for Fun

About Hunt for Fun

Do you think you have what it takes to solve mysteries? Have you felt comfortable dreaming and fantasizing about yourself in a detective’s hat? Gear up as TECHetc 2K23 brings you the ‘Hunt For Fun’ event, where players or treasure hunters search for answers to questions. Players use clues and hints given along with the question to get the answer and progress through the hunt unlocking greater treasures every time. More thrill, more excitement, more madness, more treasures!

Event Details:

The event consists of 4 rounds, and the contestants have to crack each clue of that round before moving on to the next round.
From the 1st round, only 12 groups will be selected for the next round.
The time allotted for the 1st round is 10 minutes, after which the contestants will be eliminated.


Rules:

1. Only team participation is allowed. Each team must contain only three members. However, at least one team member ought to be from HETC.
2. The event consists of 4 rounds, and the contestants have to crack each clue of that round before moving on to the next round.
3. From the 1st round, only 12 groups will be selected for the next round.
4. The time allotted for the 1st round is 10 minutes, after which the contestants will be eliminated.
5. Judges/Coordinators reserve the right to change the event’s timings and rules, which will be notified to all the participants at the latest. The decision of the jury panel will be final and binding.


Team Details:

Hunt for fun is a team event. Each team must contain only three members. However, at least one team member ought to be from HETC.


Eligibility:

All Participants should have proper College ID card on them, and should be able to produce the same in original when asked for verification, failing which they will not be allowed to participate. Each participant should be a student pursuing some field of study in college/university.


Registration Fees:

The one-time registration fee for each candidate is Rs.100/- .
The entry fee for the Hunt for Fun event is Rs.60/- per team.
