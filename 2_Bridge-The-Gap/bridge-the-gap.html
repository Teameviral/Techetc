BRIDGE THE GAP

About Bridge The Gap

Each team will be given 200 flat Popsicle sticks (ice-cream sticks) and a limited quantity of white adhesive (finical) as bridge-building material. Minimum length of the bridge is 50 cm. Bridge will be placed on an abutment with a 40 cm clear span. Load will be placed across the bridge span. Pin joints and clipping are strictly prohibited.


Rules:

1. Participants should enroll their Group No. on their respective bridge model by the volunteers after completion of the bridge.
2. Participants can use a knife, pencil and ordinary scale to build the bridge (Scissors and clips are not allowed).
3. Any unfair means will lead to the termination of the participating group.
4. Minimum clear passage of roadway along the span should be 6 cm x 6 cm (height x width).
5. Participants should provide enough space to insert two 8 mm diameter steel bars in the middle zone with a gapping of not more than 4 cm for loading.
6. Final judgment will be based on the “Maximum Ratio of Collapse load of the bridge to the Final Weight of the bridge”.
7. Decision of the judges will be final.
8. Violating any of the above rules will lead to a penalty.


Team Details:

Each group may consist of a maximum of four and minimum of two participants.


Eligibility:

All Participants should have proper College ID card on them, and should be able to produce the same in original when asked for verification, failing which they will not be allowed to participate. Each participant should be a student pursuing some field of study in college/university.


Registration Fees:

The one-time registration fee for each candidate is Rs.100/- .
The entry fee for each group is Rs.100/- only.
