TECHETC

ElectroTech Exploration

About ElectroTech Exploration

Electronics and Technology meet at every instance of life in present day. The importance of electro-technology can be well understood by the daily life usage of mobile phones and laptops specifically. This event will give the candidates a chance to innovate and explore in the field of electronics and technology.

Event Details:

The contest will consist of Two Rounds.

Each of the rounds will be Elimination Round.
The detailed description of each round is provided below:
 Round 1 (WHO AM I?) :- This round will consist of a set of 15 questions or clues based on Electronics field.

Round 2 (Tech Exploration):- In this round, a latest technical topic based on Electronics will be given. The participants will be given a stipulated time to explore and finally, they will have to present it.


Rules:

1. Judgement of event coordinators will be final.

2. Every round is time-dependent. Each round will start and end as per scheduled time. No extra time will be given.

3. If there is any “TIE”, minimum time and maximum correct answer(s) will be considered for “TIE Breaker”. Marks obtained in the 1st Round may also be considered for “RE-TIE BREAKER”.

4. After Round 1, Eight (8) participants (maximum) will sail through to Round 2.

5. Any rule or instruction is subjected to change as per requirement.


Team Details:

It is an individual event.


Eligibility:

All Participants should have proper College ID card on them, and should be able to produce the same in original when asked for verification, failing which they will not be allowed to participate. Each participant should be a student pursuing some field of study in college/university.


Registration Fees:

The one-time registration fee for each candidate is Rs.100/- .
