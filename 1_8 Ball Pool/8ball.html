8 Ball Pool

About 8 Ball Pool

8 Ball Pool is a pool game for Android that allows you to play against people from all over the world in turn-based online games to find out who the best player is. Gameplay in 8 Ball Pool is very similar to any other pool game. Use your finger to aim the cue, and swipe it forward to hit the ball in the direction that you want. From there, you need to try and beat your opponent by following the corresponding rules - hit the striped or solid balls.

Event Details:

Opponent will be selected by lottery system. Unique id must be registered with IGN(In Game Name). MOD/Hack version is not allowed. The first round of the event will be played on the London Board and the board increments to the next one as the rounds progress. Both the players will have to use the same cue (Beginner Cue) and changing the cue will result in disqualification.

• All registered players will be added to a whatsapp group, and winner of each game must share the Winning screenshot in the group.
• Device or internet related issues will not be considered after the match starts.
