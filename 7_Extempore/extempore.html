EXTEMPORE

About Extempore

Extempore means without planning or preparation. In this event, a candidate is asked to speak briefly on a given topic without preparation beforehand. Clarity of thought, range of knowledge, presence of mind and fluency of a candidate are generally examined in an Extempore.


Event Details:

Participants may speak in any of the three languages, ENGLISH, BENGALI or HINDI. Each participant will be given 4 minutes to speak on a topic picked up by him or her. A bunch of topics will be given to select anyone by means of a lottery randomly. First, second and third-place winners will be awarded mementos and certificates.

Rules:

1. A maximum of 30 seconds may be given to a candidate for preparation after knowing the topic mentioned in the lotterychit.
2. A participant will be allowed to select the topic once only.
3. Participants will not be allowed to take any paper or material with them.
4. Participation certificates will be given to all participants.
5. Judges will look for clarity of thought, range of knowledge, presence of mind, effective use of language, and body language. The decision of the Jury Panel will be final and binding.


Team Details:

This is an Individual Event.


Eligibility:

All Participants should have proper College ID card on them, and should be able to produce the same in original when asked for verification, failing which they will not be allowed to participate. Each participant should be a student pursuing some field of study in college/university.


Registration Fees:

The one-time registration fee for each candidate is Rs.100/-
