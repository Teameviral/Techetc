About Code-2-Duo

CODE-2-DUO is the completely revamped coding event of TECHETC 2k23 and will be an awesome platform to hone your programming and problem-solving skills over 2 grueling rounds. If you feel the burning desire in yourself to code, and want to prove yourselves to be among the best, come join us and code your way to glory!

Event Details:

CODE-2-DUO will consist of 2 rounds, consisting of problems, testing your logic, algorithmic and problem-solving skills. The rounds are as follows:

Round 1 (Prelims): 15 code snippets written in C language whose outputs are to be given by the participants.
Time-30 minutes.

Round 2 (Finals): 4 problem statements ranging from medium to hard level for which a solution is to be given by the teams in C / C++ / JAVA / PYTHON.
Time-1 hour.

Note: More rounds may get included based on number of participants.


Rules:

No team can open any tab on the browser other than HackerRank. Breaking this rule will result in disqualification. Also, if any team is caught researching about the question online (including looking at your own past submissions to problems other than those provided), or cheating, or communicating with other teams during the contest, involved team(s) will be disqualified. The decision of the coordinators will be final.

Rules for Round 1 (Prelims):
• Round 1 will have 15 code snippets written in C language.
• The participants need to give the outputs of the code snippets.
• There will be negative marking in this round.
⦁ In case of tie in scores, submission time will be considered.
• Time duration will be 30 minutes.
• Top 25% teams will move to next round (can be altered if required).

Rules for Round 2 (Finals):
• Since it is an online contest (under offline invigilation), start time cannot be manipulated as it has to be to set before-hand.
• Since Round 2 will also be an online contest (under offline invigilation), start time cannot be manipulated as it has to be to set before-hand. They must report at least 30 minutes before the contest.
• Round 2 will have 4 problems, ranging from medium to hard difficulty.
• Time duration will be 1 hour.

This round will be hosted on HackerRank (www.hackerrank.com) as private/closed contests, of which the link will be shared right before the contest. Languages allowed: C / C++ / JAVA / PYTHON.
OS: Linux, Windows
Browser: Chrome, Firefox, Edge
Compilers: Online HackerRank IDE.


Team Details:

CODE-2-DUO is a team event, with each team having two members. Any one member must have an account in hackerRank. If you don’t have one, get yourself an account at Hackerrank DO CARRY YOUR USERNAME and PASSWORD TO THE EVENT LOCATION ON THE EVENT DAY.


Eligibility:

All Participants should have proper College ID card on them, and should be able to produce the same in original when asked for verification, failing which they will not be allowed to participate. Each participant should be a student pursuing some field of study in college/university.

Note: Only one of the participants per group will be able to access the hackerRank IDE. Participants can discuss between themselves.


Registration Fees:

The one-time registration fee for each candidate is Rs.100/- .
